# Deuteronomy — 12-Week Small Group Bible Study

Discussion guides for a 12-week walk through the book of Deuteronomy, written for adult small groups in an evangelical church setting. The materials help a group discover the meaning of the text together through careful observation, interpretation, and application — moving from **text → meaning → theology → application**.

## What's in This Repository

Each week includes two guides:

| File | Audience | Contents |
|------|----------|----------|
| `Week N - Deuteronomy … .md` | **Group members** | Discussion questions, main theme, and brief introduction |
| `Week N - Leader's Guide.md` | **Leaders** | Icebreakers, suggested answers, follow-up prompts, and guidance on which questions to prioritize |

The full reading and discussion schedule is in [`Deuteronomy 12wk schedule.md`](Deuteronomy%2012wk%20schedule.md).

## How to Use These Guides

1. **Before the meeting**, group members read the week's assigned chapters (see the schedule).
2. **During the meeting**, work through the discussion questions in the participant guide.
3. **Leaders** should prepare using the Leader's Guide. Look for:
   - **★** — questions worth spending the most time on
   - **⚑** — questions that tend to be difficult (extra prompts are provided)

The Leader's Guide answers are a compass, not a script. The goal is for the group to discover what the text says, not for the leader to lecture.

|------|-------------------|---------------|
| 1 | none | Remember God's faithfulness |
| 2 | Deut 1–4 | The LORD alone is God |
| 3 | Deut 5–6 | Covenant love and wholehearted obedience |
| 4 | Deut 7–8 | Chosen by grace, warned against forgetting |
| 5 | Deut 9–11 | Not by your righteousness: grace that changes the heart |
| 6 | Deut 12–14 | Worship on God's terms: a holy people |
| 7 | Deut 15–18 | Open hands and faithful leaders: a people under God's Word |
| 8 | Deut 19–21 | Justice, trust, and the seriousness of covenant life |
| 9 | Deut 22–26 | Everyday holiness and a people who remember |
| 10 | Deut 27–28 | Two roads: the blessing and the curse of the covenant |
| 11 | Deut 29–30 | Choose life: the heart God must give |
| 12 | Deut 31–34 | God's Word endures beyond Moses |

See the schedule file for the specific discussion passages each week.
